Agriculture in Uttarakhand

Agricultural practices are the main stay of the people of Uttranchal. Out of the total population, more than 75% of people are engaged either with the main occupation of agriculture or its allied practices, dominated by traditional subsistence cereal farming. Among them, the main crops are rice, wheat, millet, barley, all types of pulses, all types of oilseeds and almost all types of fruits.
